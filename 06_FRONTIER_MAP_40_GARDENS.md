# 06 · FRONTIER MAP — The 40 Databases

## What this is

A prior research session (March 2026) identified 40 open / research-grade databases that together represent "one of the most important preservation projects in human history" — rescuing rare knowledge from extinction, neglect, institutional disinterest, and time. Every one of them is a candidate for a Knowledge Garden.

This document tiers all 40 by **buildability × strategic value** for us. It is the feeder pipeline for the Horizon 2 platform expansion story and the emotional/cultural counterweight to the 12 commercial verticals in the investor deck.

**Important framing:** The Frontier 40 is not where the money is. It's where the **meaning** is. It's what makes us a civilization-scale project instead of a SaaS company. These gardens give us cultural capital, press coverage, academic partnerships, grant funding, documentary material, and the moral license to charge enterprise prices on HKG and BKG. **Money gardens fund meaning gardens. Meaning gardens fund cultural dominance. Cultural dominance defends the money gardens.**

## The tiering rationale

- **Tier 1** = fastest to revenue-neutral ship, highest API maturity, strongest visual/narrative hooks, natural companions to OKG
- **Tier 2** = proven value, moderate infrastructure lift, Year 2 roadmap
- **Tier 3** = high cultural value + rights/ethics complexity; needs curatorial board
- **Tier 4** = civilization-scale prestige projects; institutional partnership required
- **Tier 5** = consciousness / fringe; brand-risky, build only after trust is established

## How the Frontier 40 supports the commercial business

1. **Cultural capital** — Nobody else in "vertical knowledge graph" is building the Extinction Garden or the Sound Garden. This is an unmatchable moat of meaning.
2. **Press and partnerships** — American Orchid Society, Cornell Lab of Ornithology, Duke MorphoSource, IUCN, Allen Institute. Each partnership is a press cycle and a credibility infusion.
3. **Grant funding** — Tier 1–4 gardens are eligible for preservation, conservation, and cultural-heritage grants the commercial gardens cannot access. Estimated $200K–$5M/year in non-dilutive funding once we're established.
4. **Documentary material** — The Bloom Ledger universe expands naturally from orchids to extinction to migration to sound. One production crew, many gardens.
5. **Founder energy replenishment** — Building HKG Admin Lane credentialing workflows is not spiritually sustaining. Building the Extinction Garden is. Chilly and the creative team need the meaning gardens to fund the human cost of the compliance gardens. **This is a real strategic consideration, not a soft one.**
6. **Recruiting** — Top engineers and designers will take pay cuts to work on the Sound Garden or the Mind Garden. They will not take pay cuts to work on hospital credentialing software.

## The one rule

**No Frontier 40 garden is built before HKG and BKG are generating MRR.** The commercial gardens fund the meaning gardens. Reversing that order is how good companies die.

Post-seed, the minimum test to start a Frontier garden is: **BKG at $100K MRR AND HKG has 10 paying enterprise customers AND the pattern deploy playbook is documented and repeatable.** Until then, the Frontier 40 stays on this document as aspirational inspiration, not execution work.
