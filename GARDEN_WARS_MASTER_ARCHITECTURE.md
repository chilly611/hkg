## VISION

Garden Wars is an interactive CEO strategy simulator for The Knowledge Gardens — a platform company building AI-native operating systems for fragmented trillion-dollar industries. It is NOT a slideshow with buttons. It is a living decision tree that helps two founders see the grand chessboard AND drill into the micro-battles, make informed choices, save paths as "hero's journeys," and evolve the tool as the business evolves.

**The metaphor:** You are planting a garden empire. Each decision is a seed. Each path is a branch. The tree grows as you play. You can prune branches (go back), graft new ones (explore alternatives), and save the full tree as a map of your strategy.

---

## THE 4 SCORES (Replace all current metrics)

Every option, every path, every saved strategy is measured on 4 axes:

| Score | What It Measures | Icon | Color |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Human Value** | How much does this help real humans? Health outcomes, knowledge preservation, lives improved, communities served | 🌱 | Green |
| **Machine Value** | How much does this strengthen the AI citation moat? Data density, MCP adoption, llms.txt coverage, API consumption | 🤖 | Purple |
| **Fun Factor** | How excited are Chilly and John to work on this? Energy, passion, creative fulfillment, recruiting appeal | 🔥 | Orange |
| **Profitability** | Revenue potential, margins, time-to-revenue, capital efficiency, investor appeal | 💰 | Gold |

Each scored 1-10. Displayed as a radar chart / diamond on every option and every saved path. The "perfect" option scores high on all 4. Most real options have tradeoffs — that's the game.

**Additional tracked metrics (secondary dashboard):**
- Projected Y1 / Y3 / Y5 ARR
- Risk level (1-10)
- Regulatory complexity (1-10)
- Time to first revenue (months)
- Team size needed
- Capital required
- Moat depth (1-10)

### Act 1: The Frontier Map (Garden Selection)

- **9 Scoped Commercial Gardens:** Legal, Education, Finance, Agriculture, Energy, Real Estate, Pharma, Insurance, Government

Each garden is a node. Active ones glow. Scoped ones are outlined. Research ones are dimmer but explorable. Tap any garden to see the full detail panel (What/How/Why/Revenue/Market/Moat/Synergy/4-Scores).

**The first decision:** Which garden do you lead with? (Or: which combination? Dual track? Triple track?)

### Act 2: Lane Selection
Once a garden is chosen, you pick which of the 4 lanes to build first:
- **Admin Lane** (Red Surface — "The Cash Engine")
- **Professional Lane** (Green Surface — "The Engagement Engine")
- **Public Lane** (Gold Surface — "The Gravity Well")
- **Machine Lane** (Purple — "The Data Moat")

Each lane has its own economics, timeline, risk profile, and 4-score breakdown.

### Act 3: Niche Drilling (THE DEEP BRANCHING)
This is where it gets real. Once you've chosen a garden + lane, you drill into SPECIFIC niches. This is where the micro-decisions live.

**Example: HKG → Patient Gravity Well → What do we target?**

### Act 4: Revenue Architecture
For the chosen niche(s), how do you monetize? Options include:
- Freemium → premium subscription
- Affiliate commissions
- Advertising (health content RPMs $15-50+)
- Enterprise SaaS
- API/data licensing
- Referral fees (telehealth, clinics, trials)
- Marketplace take rates
- Some combination

### Act 5: Capital & Team
When to raise, how much, who to hire first, partnership strategy.

### Act 6: Expansion Sequencing
Which garden next? Which lane? How does the first garden's data feed the second?

### The Trail Map (Hero's Journey)
A horizontal branching tree at the bottom of the screen that grows left-to-right as you make decisions. Each node is a choice point. Branches show the paths not taken (dimmed). The chosen path glows.

**Each node on the trail shows:**
- The decision made
- The 4-score impact (tiny radar chart or 4 colored dots)
- The ARR projection at that point
- Click to rewind to that node

**Saving a path** saves the entire tree as a "Hero's Journey" — a named strategy with:
- Full decision sequence
- 4-score trajectory over time
- ARR projection curve
- A shareable summary (email/export)

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR THE GAME

1. **The game is a DARK theme strategy tool** — this is NOT the platform brand (which is light/parchment). This is a war room. Dark backgrounds, glowing accents, gold/green/purple highlights.

2. **Every decision must feel consequential** — no throwaway choices. Each one should make the player think "wait, let me reconsider."

3. **Research is in-game, not out-of-game** — the Claude API call happens inside the game UI. The player never leaves the experience.

4. **The trail map is the hero** — it's not just navigation, it's the OUTPUT of the game. The saved hero's journey IS the deliverable.

5. **Information density scales with interest** — summary cards at the top level, full analysis on tap, deep research on demand. Progressive disclosure.

6. **Music and motion create atmosphere** — ambient soundtrack during play, staggered animations on every transition, floating background elements. It must feel alive.

7. **The 4 scores replace all other metrics as primary** — Human Value, Machine Value, Fun Factor, Profitability. Everything else is secondary.

8. **Images are everywhere** — every garden, every decision, every niche should have visual anchors. Use the 37 existing assets aggressively. Generate more if needed.

9. **See dollar projections** — ARR curves that update with every decision
10. **Feel like they've thought it through** — the game should give them MORE confidence in their choices, not less
